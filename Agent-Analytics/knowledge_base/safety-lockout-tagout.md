# Safety Lockout / Tagout (LOTO) Procedure

## Applicability
All maintenance, repair, cleaning, and setup tasks where unexpected startup could cause injury.

## Required Equipment
- Personal padlock (one per worker)
- Group lockout hasp
- Danger tag (red)
- Energy isolation devices (valve locks, breaker locks, etc.)

## Six Steps of LOTO
1. **PLAN** — Identify all energy sources (electrical, pneumatic, hydraulic, thermal, gravitational)
2. **SHUT DOWN** — Follow normal shutdown procedure
3. **ISOLATE** — Disconnect all energy sources using lockable devices
4. **LOCK & TAG** — Each worker applies their personal lock and tag
5. **VERIFY** — Attempt to start equipment (should not start), test for zero energy
6. **PERFORM WORK** — Only after verification is complete

## Removal
1. Remove tools and guards
2. Inspect area for personnel
3. Each worker removes their own lock and tag
4. Notify operators that equipment is ready to return to service

## NEVER
- Remove another person's lock
- Use someone else's lock/key
- Bypass a lockout device
- Leave a lockout point unlocked even for "just a minute"
- Start work without verifying zero energy state
