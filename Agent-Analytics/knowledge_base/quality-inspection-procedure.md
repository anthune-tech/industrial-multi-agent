# Quality Inspection Procedure

## Inspection Frequency
- First-piece inspection: Every shift start
- In-process inspection: Every 30 minutes
- Final inspection: End of batch/lot

## Sampling Plan
- AQL 1.0% normal severity per ISO 2859-1
- Sample size: square root of lot size, min 20 pieces
- Critical defects: 0 tolerance
- Major defects: ≤ 2% of sample
- Minor defects: ≤ 5% of sample

## Measurement Tools
- Caliper: ±0.02mm accuracy
- Micrometer: ±0.005mm accuracy
- Go/No-Go gauges: Check daily for wear
- CMM: Monthly calibration required

## Defect Classification
| Class | Definition | Action |
|-------|------------|--------|
| Critical | Safety hazard or function failure | Stop production immediately |
| Major | Will cause product failure in field | Segregate, 100% sort |
| Minor | Cosmetic or non-functional | Document, may continue |

## Documentation
- Record all measurements in QMS
- Attach inspection tags to accepted lots
- Red tags for rejected material
