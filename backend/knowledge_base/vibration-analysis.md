# Vibration Analysis

## Measurement Points
- Drive end bearing (vertical, horizontal, axial)
- Non-drive end bearing (vertical, horizontal)
- Foundation/base (vertical)
- Casing (horizontal)

## Frequency Ranges
| Frequency | Source | Typical Fault |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| 1x RPM | Imbalance | Unbalance in rotor |
| 2x RPM | Misalignment | Angular or parallel misalignment |
| Harmonics > 3x | Looseness | Structural or bearing looseness |
| High freq > 1kHz | Bearing | Bearing wear/defect |
| Sidebands | Gears | Gear tooth wear |

## Severity Guidelines (ISO 10816-3)
| Velocity (mm/s RMS) | Assessment | Action |
|---------------------|------------|--------|
| < 1.8 | Good | No action |
| 1.8 - 4.5 | Acceptable | Monitor |
| 4.5 - 11.2 | Unsatisfactory | Plan maintenance |
| > 11.2 | Unacceptable | Shut down & repair |

## Measurement Schedule
| Equipment Class | Frequency |
|----------------|-----------|
| Critical (e.g., main conveyors) | Weekly |
| Semi-critical | Monthly |
| Standard | Quarterly |
