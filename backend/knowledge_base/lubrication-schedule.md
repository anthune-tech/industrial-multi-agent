# Lubrication Schedule

## Lubricant Types
| Equipment | Lubricant | Grade | Interval |
|-----------|-----------|-------|----------|
| Conveyor bearings | NLGI #2 grease | Lithium complex | Weekly |
| Gearboxes | ISO VG 320 oil | Synthetic EP | 6 months |
| Hydraulic systems | ISO VG 46 oil | Anti-wear | Monthly check |
| Chains | SAE 30 oil | Non-detergent | Daily |
| Spindles | ISO VG 10 oil | Spindle oil | 2000 hours |

## Procedure
1. Clean grease fittings before applying
2. Apply grease until fresh grease exits the seal
3. Do NOT over-grease (causes overheating)
4. Use pressure-relief grease fitting adapters
5. Record lubrication date and quantity in CMMS

## Frequency Guide
- **Daily (operator)**: Chain oil, visual coolant level check
- **Weekly (maintenance)**: Bearing greasing on conveyors
- **Monthly**: Hydraulic oil sample, filter inspection
- **Quarterly**: Gearbox oil change, grease all fittings
- **Annually**: Full oil flush and replacement

## Safety
- Wear gloves when handling lubricants
- Dispose of used oil in designated containers
- Use drip trays under all lubrication points
- Report any leaks immediately
