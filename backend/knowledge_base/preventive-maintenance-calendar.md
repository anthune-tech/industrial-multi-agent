# Preventive Maintenance Calendar

## Daily (Operator)
- Visual inspection of equipment
- Check fluid levels (oil, coolant, hydraulic)
- Listen for abnormal noises
- Record production counts and issues
- Clean work area (5S)

## Weekly (Maintenance)
- Lubricate conveyor bearings
- Check belt tension and tracking
- Inspect electrical panels for loose connections
- Clean air filters on control cabinets
- Test emergency stops

## Monthly
- Calibrate critical sensors
- Change hydraulic filters
- Inspect and tighten mechanical fasteners
- Grease all fittings
- Review vibration analysis data
- Sample and test lubricating oils

## Quarterly
- Replace air filters
- Inspect and adjust chain drives
- Check motor winding insulation resistance
- Calibrate all process sensors
- Inspect structural supports and foundations
- Clean cooling towers and heat exchangers

## Semi-Annual
- Replace gearbox oil
- Inspect and clean all electrical panels
- Test all safety interlocks
- Overhaul valves and actuators
- Inspect structural steel for corrosion

## Annual
- Major equipment overhaul
- Replace all bearings on critical equipment
- Rewind or replace motors based on test results
- Full system calibration
- Update maintenance history and plan for next year
