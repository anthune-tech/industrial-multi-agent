# Conveyor Belt Maintenance

## Frequency
- Daily: Visual inspection
- Weekly: Belt tension check
- Monthly: Full lubrication and alignment
- Quarterly: Replace worn rollers

## Steps
1. Lock out / tag out (LOTO) before any maintenance
2. Inspect belt for cracks, fraying, or uneven wear
3. Check belt tension: deflection should be 1-2% of span length
4. Lubricate rollers with NLGI #2 grease
5. Verify tracking alignment +/- 2mm
6. Test emergency stop before restarting

## Common Issues
- Belt slippage → increase tension or replace worn pulley lagging
- Belt mistracking → adjust snub rollers or check frame level
- Excessive noise → check bearing condition on idler rollers
