# Emergency Stop Protocol

## Activation Conditions
- Imminent personnel injury
- Fire or smoke
- Major equipment failure
- Chemical spill
- Uncontrolled release of energy

## Immediate Actions
1. Press nearest emergency stop (red mushroom button)
2. Announce "EMERGENCY STOP — [Location]" over PA system
3. Evacuate the immediate area
4. Do NOT reset E-stop until hazard is cleared

## Reset Procedure
1. Verify the hazard has been fully resolved
2. Perform a visual inspection of the affected equipment
3. Clear all personnel from the danger zone
4. Reset the E-stop button (twist to release)
5. Press "Reset" on the control panel
6. Sound horn for 3 seconds before restart
7. Restart equipment at reduced speed first
8. Monitor for 5 minutes before returning to normal operation

## Documentation
- Record incident in the shift log
- File incident report within 1 hour
- Attach maintenance work order if repairs needed
- Supervisor must review within 24 hours

## Testing
- Test ALL e-stop buttons weekly
- Log test results in maintenance system
- Any failed e-stop = immediate work order, CRITICAL priority
